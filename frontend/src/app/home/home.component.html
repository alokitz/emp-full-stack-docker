About ALOK-IT ERP

Empowering Enterprises with Secure, Scalable & Smart Solutions

Alok-It ERP is a comprehensive enterprise resource planning system designed to streamline operations, enhance data accuracy, and ensure high-level information security across departments. It integrates modules like Payroll, HR, Finance, and Inventory under one unified platform.

Built using modern technologies — Spring Boot, Angular, and MySQL — it offers a seamless experience with Two-Factor Authentication (2FA), JWT Security, and role-based access. Our system ensures that critical data remains safe, accessible, and synchronized in real-time.
